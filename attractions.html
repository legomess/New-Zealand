Best Places to Visit

New Zealand is full of amazing places. Here are three top attractions you should not miss:

1. Milford Sound

Milford Sound is a beautiful fjord in the South Island. It has high mountains, waterfalls, and sea life. You can take a boat tour and see dolphins and seals.

2. Rotorua

Rotorua is famous for hot springs, geysers, and Māori culture. You can relax in thermal pools and watch traditional performances.

3. Hobbiton

Visit the movie set from The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit. Walk through the green hills and see the tiny hobbit houses in Matamata.
