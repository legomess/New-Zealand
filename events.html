Popular Events and Festivals

New Zealand hosts many fun events every year. These are some of the most famous:

Waitangi Day

Held on February 6th, this national holiday remembers the signing of the Treaty of Waitangi in 1840. People celebrate with music, food, and Māori performances.

Pasifika Festival

This is one of the biggest festivals in Auckland. It celebrates Pacific Island cultures with dance, music, and food from different islands like Samoa, Tonga, and Fiji.

Queenstown Winter Festival

This is a fun event in June to welcome the winter season. It includes snow sports, fireworks, music, and food. It’s great for families and tourists.
